  • Lechuguilla Cave, made famous on the Planet Earth series, is located in Carlsbad Caverns National Park. It cannot be accessed by the public, and entry is allowed only for special research purposes.

Enchanted Rock

  • Enchanted Rock stands 425 feet above its base, and its highest point is 1,825 feet above sea level
  • Both structures were formed with Town Mountain Granite, which is a mix of salmon-pink feldspar with flecks of black mica, quartz, biotite and other minerals
  • While this is just a legend, Tonkawa Indians believed ghosts inhabited the area because creaking sounds came from the dome.
  • While Earth is estimated to be 4.5 billion years old, Enchanted Rock formed when two continents collided over one billion years ago.
